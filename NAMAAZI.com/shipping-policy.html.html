🚚 Free shipping on all orders above ₹1,499 within India. Orders are processed Monday–Saturday, excluding public holidays. We partner with trusted couriers for fast, safe delivery.

1. Order Processing Time

Once your payment is confirmed, our team begins preparing your order. Please allow:

  • Standard items: 1–2 business days processing before dispatch.
  • Embroidered/festive items: 2–3 business days processing due to quality checks.
  • Custom or made-to-measure orders: 5–7 business days processing.

You will receive a dispatch confirmation email with your tracking number once your order ships.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an email and SMS with a unique tracking number and a direct link to track your shipment in real time.

  • You can also track your order by visiting the My Orders section on our website.
  • Tracking information may take up to 12 hours to update after dispatch.
  • For WhatsApp tracking updates, message us at +91 XXXXX XXXXX.

5. Delays & Shipping Issues

While we strive for timely delivery, delays may occasionally occur due to:

  • Natural calamities, floods, or strike action affecting courier operations.
  • High-volume festive seasons (Eid, Diwali, Christmas).
  • Incorrect or incomplete address provided by the customer.
  • Customs clearance for international shipments.

If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated window, please wait an additional 2 business days before contacting us. Delays beyond 10 days (domestic) or 21 days (international) will be investigated and resolved with priority.

6. Failed & Undeliverable Deliveries

If your delivery fails due to an incorrect address, customer unavailability (after 3 attempts), or refusal of delivery:

  • The package will be returned to our warehouse.
  • Re-delivery charges will apply (₹99 domestic, actual cost international).
  • If the customer requests a refund on a returned package, shipping costs and a 5% restocking fee will be deducted.

Please ensure your delivery address and phone number are accurate at the time of ordering.

7. Festive Season Shipping Notice

🎉 During Eid, Diwali, and major festivals, order volumes increase significantly. Please order at least 7–10 days in advance to ensure timely delivery. Express shipping is recommended during these periods.
