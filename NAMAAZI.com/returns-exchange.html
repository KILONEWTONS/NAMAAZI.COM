💚 We want you to love what you wear. If something isn't right, we offer 15-day hassle-free returns and free size exchanges on all standard orders within India.

1. Overview

At NAMAAZI, we stand behind the quality of every piece we sell. Our return and exchange window is 15 calendar days from the date of delivery. Items must be in their original condition — unworn, unwashed, with all tags attached — to be eligible.

We do not charge a return shipping fee for orders within India when the return is due to a sizing issue or our error. For change-of-mind returns, a nominal ₹99 reverse pickup charge may apply.

2. What Can & Cannot Be Returned

✅ Eligible for Return

  • Items in original, unworn condition
  • All tags and packaging intact
  • Returned within 15 days of delivery
  • Incorrect item received
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Size mismatch from size chart

❌ Not Eligible for Return

  • Worn, washed, or altered items
  • Items without original tags
  • Custom or made-to-measure pieces
  • Sale items (clearly marked final sale)
  • Accessories (Tasbeeh, Prayer Mats)
  • Returns requested after 15 days

3. Return Process — Step by Step

1

Initiate Return Request

Email returns@namaazi.com or WhatsApp us with your order number and reason for return. Within 24 hours on business days.

2

Receive Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return label (India only) or confirm a reverse pickup date within 1–2 business days.

3

Pack & Dispatch

Securely pack the item in its original packaging. Keep your courier receipt as proof of dispatch.

4

Quality Check

Once received, our team inspects the item within 2–3 business days. You'll receive email confirmation.

5

Refund or Exchange Processed

Refund issued within 5–7 business days. Exchange dispatched within 2–3 business days after inspection.

4. Size Exchange

Not the right fit? We offer free size exchanges on all eligible items within India, subject to stock availability.

  • You can exchange for a different size of the same product only.
  • Exchange is permitted once per order.
  • If the requested size is unavailable, we will issue a store credit valid for 12 months.
  • International customers: size exchanges attract standard return shipping costs.

📏 To avoid sizing issues, please refer to our Size Guide on every product page before ordering. Our garments follow standard Indian sizing (XS to 3XL).

6. Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Item

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, we sincerely apologise. Please:

  • Email us at support@namaazi.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Attach clear photographs of the item, the packaging, and the order label.
  • We will arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or full refund — your choice.
  • No return shipping charge will apply for these cases.

7. International Returns

For international orders, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked courier service. NAMAAZI is not liable for items lost in transit during return.

  • Please mark the package as "Return of Goods — No Commercial Value" to avoid customs charges.
  • Once received and inspected, a refund (minus original shipping) will be issued within 10 business days.
  • Customs duties or taxes paid by the customer are non-refundable.
